# The Compounding Philosophy

Most AI marketing tools are prompt libraries. They give you templates, you fill in blanks, you get generic output.

Compounding Marketing is different.

## Why "Compounding"?

Every skill builds on the others. Your positioning informs your copy. Your copy informs your outreach. Your outreach results inform your positioning.

```
positioning → copy → landing pages → ads → outreach → feedback → positioning
```

The context compounds. The AI gets smarter about your business with every interaction.

## Research-Backed Frameworks

We don't make up marketing advice. Every skill is built on proven frameworks from practitioners who've done the work:

- **Positioning:** April Dunford's Obviously Awesome methodology
- **Copywriting:** Joanna Wiebe's conversion copywriting principles
- **Awareness:** Eugene Schwartz's breakthrough advertising levels
- **Outreach:** Chris Voss negotiation tactics applied to sales
- **GTM:** The Reforge growth model

## Deliverables, Not Fluff

Each skill outputs something you can use:
- A positioning document
- Ad copy variations
- An outreach sequence
- A landing page structure

Not "10 tips for better marketing." Actual artifacts you can ship.

## Context is Everything

The `.cm-context` file isn't a nice-to-have. It's the foundation.

A generic AI gives generic output. An AI that knows your ICP, competitors, and voice gives output that sounds like you wrote it.

## Compound Your Intelligence

The more you use it, the better it gets:

1. **Week 1:** Run positioning. Get a positioning doc.
2. **Week 2:** Run copy. It uses your positioning automatically.
3. **Week 3:** Run outreach. It uses your copy and positioning.
4. **Week 4:** Run CRO. It optimizes based on everything before.

That's compounding.
