# Webinar Strategy

Plan high-converting webinars for lead gen and education.

## Webinar Types

**Lead Gen:**
- Topic: Industry insights, how-to
- Goal: Capture emails
- Pitch: Soft (end with CTA)

**Product Demo:**
- Topic: Product walkthrough
- Goal: Convert trials
- Pitch: Hard (product-focused)

**Customer Training:**
- Topic: Advanced features, best practices
- Goal: Retention, expansion
- Pitch: Upsell opportunities

## Process

### Step 1: Choose Topic
**Good topics:**
- Solve a real problem
- Not overtly promotional
- Featuring guest expert (boosts registration)

**Bad topics:**
- "Introduction to [Product]" (boring)
- Too broad ("Marketing 101")

### Step 2: Promote Webinar
**2 weeks before:**
- Landing page (Zoom, Webflow)
- Email to list
- Social posts
- Partner promotion (if co-hosted)

**1 week before:**
- Reminder email
- Retargeting ads

**1 day before:**
- Final reminder email

### Step 3: Run Webinar
**Structure (60 min):**
- 0-5 min: Intro, housekeeping
- 5-40 min: Content (teach, don't pitch)
- 40-50 min: Product tie-in (show how product helps)
- 50-60 min: Q&A, CTA

**Tips:**
- Use polls (increase engagement)
- Chat moderation (answer questions)
- Record (repurpose content)

### Step 4: Follow Up
**Attendees:**
- Send recording + slides
- Offer demo or trial

**No-shows:**
- Send recording
- Invite to next webinar

## Output
Webinar plan (topic, promotion timeline, run-of-show, follow-up).
