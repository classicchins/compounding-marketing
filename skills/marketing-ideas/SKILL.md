# SaaS Marketing Ideas (140+ Tactics)

Tactical marketing ideas across the customer lifecycle.

## Acquisition (Get Users)

**Content:**
- SEO blog posts
- YouTube tutorials
- Podcast appearances
- Guest posts on industry blogs
- Comparison pages ("X vs Y")
- Alternative pages ("X alternative")
- Programmatic SEO (location, category pages)

**Paid:**
- Google Search Ads
- LinkedIn Ads (B2B)
- Facebook/Instagram Ads (B2C)
- Reddit Ads
- Sponsored newsletters
- Retargeting campaigns

**Community:**
- Answer questions on Quora, Reddit
- Join industry Slack/Discord communities
- Host Twitter Spaces / Clubhouse rooms
- Speak at conferences

**Partnerships:**
- Integration partnerships
- Co-host webinars
- Affiliate program
- Reseller partnerships

**Viral/Social:**
- Free tool (calculator, grader)
- Referral program
- Social sharing prompts
- Meme marketing (if brand fits)

## Activation (Get Users to Value)

**Onboarding:**
- Welcome email sequence
- In-app onboarding checklist
- Setup wizard
- Video tutorials
- 1-on-1 onboarding calls (high-touch)
- AI chatbot assistant

**Engagement:**
- Push notifications (mobile)
- Email nudges (feature adoption)
- Empty state prompts (guide next action)
- Gamification (badges, streaks)

## Retention (Keep Users)

**Product:**
- Feature announcements (email, in-app)
- Product updates blog
- User feedback loop (surveys, interviews)
- Community (Slack, Discord)

**Content:**
- Educational webinars
- Best practices guides
- Customer spotlight interviews
- Quarterly business reviews (QBRs)

**Support:**
- Proactive outreach (usage decline)
- Help center / docs
- Live chat support
- Office hours (live Q&A)

**Loyalty:**
- Customer advisory board
- Early access to new features
- Exclusive swag
- Annual customer conference

## Revenue (Increase $ per User)

**Upsell:**
- In-app upgrade prompts (hit limit)
- Usage-based pricing (grows with them)
- Annual plan discount
- Feature comparisons (what they're missing)

**Cross-Sell:**
- Add-ons (premium features)
- Professional services (consulting, training)

**Pricing:**
- Price increases (grandfather old customers)
- Value-based pricing (align price to value)
- Tiered packaging (good, better, best)

## Process

### Step 1: Brainstorm
Review list above, note ideas that fit your business.

### Step 2: Prioritize
**ICE Framework:**
- **Impact:** How much will this move the needle? (1-10)
- **Confidence:** How sure are you it'll work? (1-10)
- **Ease:** How easy to implement? (1-10)

**Score = (Impact × Confidence × Ease) / 1000**

Sort by score, prioritize top 10.
