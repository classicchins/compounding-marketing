# Popup & Modal Optimization

Design popups that convert without annoying users.

## Types
- **Email capture:** Newsletter signup
- **Exit intent:** Last chance offer
- **Timed popup:** After X seconds
- **Scroll popup:** After scrolling Y%

## Best Practices
1. **Clear value** ("Get weekly marketing tips")
2. **Simple ask** (email only, not 5 fields)
3. **Easy dismiss** (visible X button)
4. **Timing matters** (not immediate — wait 30s+)
5. **Mobile-friendly** (full screen on mobile OK)
6. **Frequency cap** (don't show again for 30 days)

## Avoid
- Popup on arrival (annoying)
- Hard to close (dark pattern)
- Vague offer ("Subscribe for updates")

## Output
Popup copy, design recommendations, trigger settings, A/B test ideas.
