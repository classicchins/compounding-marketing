# Community Strategy

Build engaged communities that drive retention and advocacy.

## Platforms
- **Slack:** Professional, integrations
- **Discord:** Casual, gaming/tech-friendly
- **Circle/Discourse:** Forum-style
- **LinkedIn/Facebook Groups:** Discovery-friendly

## Process

### Step 1: Define Purpose
**Why a community?**
- Product support (help each other)
- Networking (connect peers)
- Feedback (shape product)
- Learning (share best practices)

### Step 2: Choose Platform
Based on audience and purpose.

### Step 3: Seed with Content
**Don't launch empty.**
- Invite 50-100 engaged users first
- Post valuable content daily (first 30 days)
- Facilitate introductions

### Step 4: Moderation & Engagement
- Set community guidelines
- Hire/assign community manager
- Celebrate wins (shout-outs)
- Regular events (AMAs, office hours)

### Step 5: Measure Success
- Active members (daily/weekly)
- Engagement rate (% posting)
- Retention (returning members)
- NPS (community satisfaction)

## Output
Community strategy (platform, purpose, seeding plan, engagement tactics).

## Moderation and Engagement Tactics

### Community Health Metrics

Track these to measure community vitality:

| Metric | Definition | Healthy Benchmark | Warning Sign |
|--------|------------|-------------------|--------------|
| **DAU/MAU** | Daily Active Users / Monthly Active Users | >20% (1 in 5 visit daily) | <10% (low engagement) |
| **Post frequency** | Posts per day | 5-10/day for 100-member community | <1/day (silence spiral) |
| **Response rate** | % of posts that get replies | >80% (someone responds to almost everything) | <50% (people feel ignored) |
| **Response time** | Avg time to first reply | <2 hours | >24 hours (slow, feels dead) |
| **Retention (30-day)** | % of new members still active after 30 days | >40% | <20% (bad onboarding) |

**How to track:**
- **DAU/MAU:** Platform analytics (Slack, Discord, Circle all provide this)
- **Post frequency:** Manual count or analytics dashboard
- **Response rate:** `(Posts with ≥1 reply) / (Total posts)`
- **Response time:** Average time between post and first reply
- **Retention:** Cohort analysis (Day 0 signups → Day 30 still active)

### Moderation Framework: What to Remove, What to Nurture

**Moderation = Gardening.** Prune bad weeds, water good plants.

#### Remove Immediately (Zero Tolerance)

| Offense | Action | Example |
|---------|--------|---------|
| **Spam** | Delete + ban | "Buy followers cheap!" |
| **Self-promo (repeated)** | Warning → delete → ban | "Check out my product" every day |
| **Harassment** | Delete + ban | Personal attacks, doxxing |
| **Off-topic (extreme)** | Delete + redirect | Political rants in marketing community |
| **Misinformation** | Delete + correct | "SEO is dead" (provably false) |

**How to enforce:**
- Set clear rules (pin to #welcome or #rules channel)
- Use automod tools (Discord/Slack bots flag spam keywords)
- Have 2-3 active mods (not just you)

---

#### Nurture (Encourage More of This)

| Behavior | How to Nurture | Example |
|----------|----------------|---------|
| **Asking questions** | Respond quickly, thank them | "Great question! Here's how..." |
| **Sharing wins** | React with emoji, ask follow-up | "🎉 Congrats! How did you do it?" |
| **Helping others** | Public recognition, badge/role | "MVP of the week: @Sarah" |
| **Sharing resources** | Pin to channel, thank publicly | "This is gold. Pinning it." |
| **Starting discussions** | Engage deeply, ask others to chime in | "What do you all think? @channel" |

**Why this matters:** People repeat behaviors that get rewarded. Reward helpfulness, not self-promotion.

### Seed Content Strategy: First 30 Days with No Members

**Problem:** Empty community feels dead. No one wants to be the first to post.

**Solution:** Seed the community yourself before launch.

#### Pre-Launch Seeding (Days -7 to 0)

**Week before launch:**

1. **Create 10-15 posts yourself:**
   - 3 questions: "What's your biggest [pain point]?"
   - 3 resources: "Here's a guide I use for [topic]"
   - 2 discussion starters: "Hot take: [opinion]. Agree or disagree?"
   - 2 polls: "Which [option] do you prefer? A or B?"
   - 5 tips/lessons: "Lesson I learned this week: [insight]"

2. **Invite 5-10 beta members** (friends, power users, early customers)
   - Ask them to post + comment before public launch
   - Seed 5-10 replies across your posts

**Launch day:**
- Community looks active (15 posts, 10 replies)
- New members see activity → feel safe to post

---

#### Post-Launch Seeding (Days 1-30)

**Daily posting schedule (you + mods):**

- **Monday:** Weekly goals thread ("What are you working on this week?")
- **Tuesday:** Share a resource (blog post, tool, template)
- **Wednesday:** Ask a question ("How do you handle [problem]?")
- **Thursday:** Success story ("Who had a win this week?")
- **Friday:** Fun/casual ("What are you reading/watching this weekend?")

**Why daily?** Consistency signals "this community is alive." Members check back.

### Engagement Loops: Challenges, AMAs, Member Spotlights

#### 1. Weekly Challenges (Drive Participation)

**Format:**
- **Monday:** Post challenge ("This week: Write a LinkedIn post using [framework]")
- **Friday:** Share results thread ("Drop your post below!")
- **Best post wins:** Shoutout, badge, or small prize

**Example (Marketing Community):**
- Challenge: "Write a cold email using the PAS framework (Problem-Agitate-Solution)"
- Friday thread: Members share their emails
- Community votes on best email
- Winner gets "Copywriter of the Week" badge

**Why it works:** Gamification + accountability + social proof

---

#### 2. Monthly AMAs (Bring in Experts)

**Format:**
- Invite guest expert (founder, thought leader, industry vet)
- Announce 1 week ahead ("Next Friday: AMA with [Name], [Title] at [Company]")
- Pin AMA thread
- Guest answers questions for 1-2 hours

**Example guests (Marketing SaaS community):**
- CEO of competing SaaS ("How we grew to $10M ARR")
- Marketing influencer ("My content strategy secrets")
- Customer success story ("How we 3x'd conversions")

**Why it works:** Exclusive access (can't get this elsewhere), drives traffic, attracts new members

---

#### 3. Member Spotlights (Make People Feel Seen)

**Format:**
- Every 2 weeks, spotlight 1 active member
- Post interview: "Meet [Name], [Job Title]. Here's their story..."
- Ask 5 questions:
  1. What do you do?
  2. Biggest challenge right now?
  3. Recent win?
  4. Favorite tool/resource?
  5. Advice for someone in your shoes?

**Why it works:** Members feel valued, others see "I could be spotlighted too" (drives participation)

### Preventing Community Death (Silence Spiral Prevention)

**Silence spiral = No one posts → Community feels dead → More people leave → Even less posting → Death.**

#### Early Warning Signs

- Post frequency drops below 1/day
- Response rate drops below 50%
- No posts from members (only from you/mods)
- New member retention <20%

#### How to Break the Spiral

**Tactic 1: Forced participation (polls, questions)**
- Post a poll daily ("A or B?")
- Tag 3-5 people: "What do you think, @Sarah @Mike @John?"
- People are more likely to answer a poll than write a long post

**Tactic 2: Bring back power users**
- DM your most active members: "Haven't seen you in a while. Everything ok?"
- Personal outreach works (they feel missed)

**Tactic 3: Run a "comeback" challenge**
- "Let's revive this community. Everyone share 1 win from this month."
- Create urgency: "Next 48 hours only"

**Tactic 4: Pivot or shut down**
- If community is truly dead (no activity for 30+ days), consider:
  - Pivot to different format (Slack → newsletter)
  - Merge with another community
  - Shut down gracefully (tell members why, archive content)

**Don't drag out a zombie community.** It's better to shut down and try again than maintain a graveyard.

## Quality Bar for Moderation and Engagement

- **Health metrics tracked:** DAU/MAU, post frequency, response rate, retention (monthly check)
- **Moderation rules clear:** Pinned rules, zero-tolerance list documented
- **Seed content strategy executed:** 10-15 posts before launch, daily posts for first 30 days
- **Engagement loops running:** At least 1 of (challenges, AMAs, spotlights) per month
- **Silence spiral prevention:** Early warning triggers defined, response plan ready
