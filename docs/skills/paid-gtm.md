## `product-hunt-launch` — Product Hunt Playbook

### Example Launch Plan

```markdown
# Product Hunt Launch Plan: AuthorityMax

## Pre-Launch (4 weeks before)

**Week 1-2: Build Relationships**
- Engage with PH community daily (upvote, comment)
- DM 10 top hunters (introduce product, ask for advice)
- Join PH Ship to build pre-launch audience

**Week 3: Prepare Assets**
- Product Hunt tagline: "Your LinkedIn ghostwriter that sounds like you"
- Gallery images (5): Hero screenshot, voice training UI, engagement prediction, testimonial, pricing
- Demo video (60s max): Problem → solution → demo
- First comment: Founder story + link to special PH offer

**Week 4: Hunter Outreach**
- Reach out to 3-5 hunters (ideally with SaaS portfolio)
- Pitch: "AI personal branding tool for founders. 1,200+ users. Interested in hunting?"

---

## Launch Day

**Time:** Launch at 12:01 AM PT (maximize 24hr window)

**Hour 1-3 (12 AM - 3 AM PT):**
- Founder posts first comment (story + offer)
- Reply to every comment within 10 min
- Share on Twitter, LinkedIn (link to PH page)

**Hour 4-8 (morning PT):**
- Email list: "We're live on Product Hunt!"
- DM top 50 users: "Would love your support on PH today"
- Continue replying to all comments

**Hour 9-24:**
- Monitor ranking (goal: Top 5 of the day)
- Reply to all comments (response rate = credibility)
- Share milestone updates ("We hit #3! Thank you!")

---

## Post-Launch

**Day 2-7:**
- Reply to all remaining comments
- Email everyone who upvoted (thank you + offer extended)
- Write launch post-mortem (share learnings)

**Success Metrics:**
- Target: Top 5 Product of the Day
- Expected: 300-500 upvotes, 50-80 new signups, 20-30 backlinks (from PH + launch coverage)
```

## `gtm-strategy` — Go-to-Market Motion

### Example (AuthorityMax)

```markdown
# GTM Strategy: AuthorityMax

## GTM Motion: Product-Led Growth (PLG)

**Rationale:**
- Low price point ($49/mo) = self-serve works
- Product value is immediately clear (generate post in 60s)
- Founders prefer to try before buying (not sales calls)

---

## Acquisition Channels (Prioritized)

| Channel | Priority | Rationale | Budget | Expected CAC |
|---------|----------|-----------|--------|--------------|
| **LinkedIn Ads** | P0 | Target audience lives on LinkedIn | $2k/mo | $100-$130 |
| **SEO** | P0 | Long-term, low CAC | $0 (time) | $20-$30 |
| **Product Hunt** | P1 | One-time launch, high visibility | $0 | $10-$20 |
| **Founder Communities** | P1 | High intent, low cost | $500/mo | $50-$80 |
| **Cold Outreach** | P2 | High effort, moderate ROI | $0 (time) | $40-$60 |

---

## Conversion Funnel

**Awareness → Trial → Activation → Paid**

1. **Awareness:** LinkedIn ad / SEO / PH
2. **Trial:** Free 7-day trial (no credit card)
3. **Activation:** Generate first post (aha moment)
4. **Paid:** Upgrade to Pro ($49/mo)

**Current Conversion Rates:**
- Ad → Trial: 5%
- Trial → Activation: 65%
- Activation → Paid: 15%
- **Overall:** 0.5% (awareness → paid)

**Improvement Targets:**
- Trial → Activation: 65% → 75% (onboarding improvements)
- Activation → Paid: 15% → 20% (email sequence optimization)
```
