# Go-to-Market Strategy

Design the right GTM motion for your product and market.

## GTM Motions

### Product-Led Growth (PLG)
**Fit:**
- Low price point (<$100/mo)
- Low complexity (self-serve)
- Fast time-to-value (<1 hour)
- End-user buyer (not executive)

**Funnel:**
Visitor → Signup → Activation → Conversion → Expansion

### Sales-Led
**Fit:**
- High price point (>$1,000/mo)
- High complexity (implementation required)
- Long time-to-value (weeks/months)
- Executive buyer

**Funnel:**
Visitor → Lead → MQL → SQL → Opportunity → Close

### Hybrid (PLG + Sales)
**Fit:**
- Mid-market price ($100-$1,000/mo)
- Self-serve for SMB, sales for enterprise
- Product qualified leads (PQL) trigger sales

**Funnel:**
Free → Self-serve conversion → Upgrade triggers sales
