# Pricing Strategy & Optimization

Design effective pricing and packaging.

## Pricing Psychology

### 1. Anchoring
Show highest price first to anchor perception.

### 2. Decoy Pricing
Add a middle tier to make top tier look reasonable.

### 3. Charm Pricing
$99 vs. $100 (perceived value difference)

### 4. Annual Discount
"Save 20% with annual billing"

## Process

### Step 1: Define Tiers (3-4 ideal)
- **Starter:** Entry point, limited features
- **Professional:** Most popular, core features
- **Enterprise:** Advanced, custom pricing

### Step 2: Feature Bundling
- Most valuable features in mid-tier
- Avoid feature bloat in top tier

### Step 3: Pricing Page Design
- Highlight most popular plan
- Clear feature comparison table
- Social proof (logos, testimonials)
- FAQ (address objections)
- CTAs: "Start Free Trial" or "Contact Sales"

### Step 4: Test
- Price points (A/B test)
- Annual vs. monthly prominence
- Feature emphasis

## Output
Pricing tier recommendations, pricing page copy, feature matrix, A/B test ideas.
