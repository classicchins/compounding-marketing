# Community Strategy

Build engaged communities that drive retention and advocacy.

## Platforms
- **Slack:** Professional, integrations
- **Discord:** Casual, gaming/tech-friendly
- **Circle/Discourse:** Forum-style
- **LinkedIn/Facebook Groups:** Discovery-friendly

## Process

### Step 1: Define Purpose
**Why a community?**
- Product support (help each other)
- Networking (connect peers)
- Feedback (shape product)
- Learning (share best practices)

### Step 2: Choose Platform
Based on audience and purpose.

### Step 3: Seed with Content
**Don't launch empty.**
- Invite 50-100 engaged users first
- Post valuable content daily (first 30 days)
- Facilitate introductions

### Step 4: Moderation & Engagement
- Set community guidelines
- Hire/assign community manager
- Celebrate wins (shout-outs)
- Regular events (AMAs, office hours)

### Step 5: Measure Success
- Active members (daily/weekly)
- Engagement rate (% posting)
- Retention (returning members)
- NPS (community satisfaction)

## Output
Community strategy (platform, purpose, seeding plan, engagement tactics).
