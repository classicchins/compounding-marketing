# Video Marketing Strategy

Plan and script video content for marketing goals.

## Video Types

**Explainer Video:**
- Problem → Solution → How it Works → CTA
- Length: 60-90 seconds
- Use: Homepage, landing pages

**Product Demo:**
- Feature walkthrough
- Length: 2-5 minutes
- Use: Product pages, sales enablement

**Customer Testimonial:**
- Customer story (Before → After)
- Length: 60-120 seconds
- Use: Social proof, case study pages

**Thought Leadership:**
- Industry insights, trends
- Length: 3-10 minutes
- Use: YouTube, LinkedIn, awareness

## Process

### Step 1: Choose Video Type & Goal
- What are you trying to achieve?
- Where will it be used?

### Step 2: Script the Video

**Explainer script structure:**
```
[0-5s] Hook: Problem statement
[5-20s] Agitate: Why it matters
[20-50s] Solution: How product solves it
[50-70s] How it works: Key features/benefits
[70-90s] CTA: Next step
```

### Step 3: Plan Visuals
- Screen recordings
- Animation
- Live action
- Graphics/text overlays

### Step 4: Distribution Plan
- Where to publish (YouTube, LinkedIn, website)
- How to promote (email, social, ads)
- Embed locations (homepage, landing pages)

## Production Basics: Minimum Viable Setup vs. Full Production

### Minimum Viable Video Setup (Budget: $0-$500)

**Equipment:**
- **Camera:** Smartphone (iPhone 12+, Samsung S21+) with 4K capability
- **Microphone:** Smartphone built-in OR $30 lavalier mic (RØDE smartLav+)
- **Lighting:** Natural window light + $40 ring light (10-12 inch) from Amazon
- **Tripod:** $25 phone tripod or desktop stand
- **Editing:** Free tools (see below)

**Best for:**
- Quick product demos
- Internal training videos
- Social media shorts
- Founder talking-head videos
- Testimonial interviews (Zoom recording)

**Quality benchmark:** YouTube tutorial quality (clean audio, decent lighting, stable framing)

---

### Mid-Level Setup (Budget: $1,000-$3,000)

**Equipment:**
- **Camera:** Mirrorless camera (Sony a6400, Canon M50) OR 4K webcam (Logitech Brio)
- **Microphone:** USB condenser mic (Blue Yeti, $130) OR XLR mic (Shure SM7B, $400 + interface)
- **Lighting:** 3-point lighting kit ($200-$400)
- **Tripod:** Fluid-head tripod for smooth panning
- **Editing:** Paid tools (Adobe Premiere, Final Cut Pro)

**Best for:**
- Polished product demos
- Thought leadership series
- YouTube channel content
- High-quality webinars

**Quality benchmark:** Professional YouTube channel / LinkedIn course quality

---

### Full Production (Budget: $5,000+ per video)

**What you get:**
- Professional video crew (director, cinematographer, editor)
- Location scouting / studio rental
- Talent (actors, voiceover artists)
- Motion graphics / animation
- Color grading, sound design
- Music licensing

**Best for:**
- Homepage hero videos
- Major product launches
- Brand commercials
- High-stakes sales videos

**Quality benchmark:** Brand video / Super Bowl ad quality

**When to outsource:** When brand reputation is on the line OR internal team can't deliver needed quality

## Script Structure by Video Type

### Tutorial / How-To Video (3-7 Minutes)

**Structure:**

**[0-10s] Hook:**
- "In this video, I'll show you how to [achieve outcome] in [timeframe]"
- Visual: Show end result (build curiosity)

**[10-60s] Problem/Context:**
- "If you've ever struggled with [pain point], this is for you"
- Visual: Show the problem (relatable)

**[60s-End] Step-by-Step Tutorial:**
- "Step 1: [Action]" → Screen recording with voiceover
- "Step 2: [Action]" → Screen recording with voiceover
- "Step 3: [Action]" → Screen recording with voiceover

**[Final 15s] CTA:**
- "If this helped, subscribe for more" (YouTube)
- "Try it yourself: [link]" (landing page)

**Example:** "How to Set Up Google Analytics 4 in 5 Minutes"

---

### Testimonial / Case Study Video (60-120 Seconds)

**Structure:**

**[0-5s] Hook:**
- Customer logo + name + result
- Visual: Customer headshot + text overlay

**[5-20s] Before (Problem):**
- "Before [Product], we struggled with [pain point]"
- Visual: Customer talking head

**[20-50s] Solution:**
- "We started using [Product] to [solution]"
- Visual: Product screen recording OR customer using product

**[50-90s] After (Result):**
- "Now we've achieved [specific result]"
- Visual: Customer talking head + stats on screen

**[90-120s] CTA:**
- "Learn more at [URL]"
- Visual: Product logo + CTA

**Example:** "How Acme Corp Reduced Churn by 35% with [Product]"

---

### Product Demo Video (2-5 Minutes)

**Structure:**

**[0-10s] Hook:**
- "See how [Product] helps you [core benefit]"
- Visual: Product UI hero shot

**[10-30s] Setup the Problem:**
- "Most [job title] struggle with [pain point]"
- Visual: Frustrated user (stock footage or animation)

**[30s-4min] Feature Walkthrough:**
- "Here's how it works…"
- Screen recording with voiceover:
  - Feature 1: Show core workflow
  - Feature 2: Show key differentiator
  - Feature 3: Show result/output

**[4-5min] CTA:**
- "Start your free trial today: [URL]"
- Visual: CTA button + product logo

**Example:** "Introducing [Feature]: The Fastest Way to [Benefit]"

## Editing Workflow & Tools

### Quick Edits (5-15 Min Editing Time)

**Use when:**
- Social media shorts (Instagram Reels, TikTok, YouTube Shorts)
- Internal updates
- Quick product demos

**Tools:**

**CapCut (Free, Mobile + Desktop):**
- Best for: Short-form social video (15-60s)
- Features: Auto-captions, trending templates, music library
- Export: Vertical (9:16) for TikTok/Reels, horizontal (16:9) for YouTube

**Loom (Free tier, then $8/mo):**
- Best for: Screen recordings + talking head
- Features: Instant recording, auto-hosting, shareable link
- Export: MP4 download (for embedding elsewhere)

**iMovie (Free, Mac only):**
- Best for: Basic cuts, titles, transitions
- Features: Simple timeline, royalty-free music
- Export: 1080p or 4K

---

### Advanced Edits (1-3 Hour Editing Time)

**Use when:**
- YouTube videos
- Thought leadership series
- Polished product demos

**Tools:**

**Descript ($12-$24/mo):**
- Best for: Editing via transcript (cut words, not timelines)
- Features: Auto-transcription, filler word removal, AI voice cloning
- Use case: Podcast editing, interview videos, spoken-word tutorials

**DaVinci Resolve (Free):**
- Best for: Color grading, professional editing
- Features: Hollywood-grade color tools, multi-track audio, effects
- Learning curve: Steeper (worth it for serious creators)

**Adobe Premiere Pro ($20/mo):**
- Best for: Professional YouTube creators, agencies
- Features: Industry-standard, integrates with After Effects (motion graphics)
- Export: Every format imaginable

---

### Editing Checklist

- [ ] **Cut dead air** (silence >2 seconds unless intentional)
- [ ] **Remove filler words** ("um," "uh," "like") — Descript does this automatically
- [ ] **Add captions** (80% of social videos watched on mute) — CapCut or Descript auto-generates
- [ ] **Add b-roll** (visual variety every 5-10 seconds — screen recordings, stock footage, graphics)
- [ ] **Add music** (subtle background track, not overpowering) — Epidemic Sound, Artlist, or YouTube Audio Library
- [ ] **Color grade** (adjust brightness/contrast for consistency)
- [ ] **Add CTA end screen** (5-10s outro with logo, CTA, subscribe button)
- [ ] **Export in correct format:**
  - YouTube: 1080p MP4, 16:9 aspect ratio
  - Instagram Reels/TikTok: 1080x1920 MP4, 9:16 vertical
  - LinkedIn: 1080p MP4, 16:9 or 1:1 square

## Distribution Checklist

### YouTube

**Pre-Upload:**
- [ ] Optimized title (keyword-rich, <60 characters)
- [ ] Thumbnail designed (1280x720px, high contrast, text overlay)
- [ ] Description written (first 150 chars = preview, include keywords + links)
- [ ] Tags added (10-15 relevant tags)
- [ ] Video file named correctly (not "Final_v3_export.mp4")

**Post-Upload:**
- [ ] Add chapters (timestamps in description OR YouTube's auto-chapters)
- [ ] Pin first comment (ask a question or provide context)
- [ ] Share link on Twitter, LinkedIn, email newsletter
- [ ] Embed on blog (if relevant)

---

### LinkedIn

**Format:**
- Native upload (not YouTube link) — higher reach
- Square (1:1) or horizontal (16:9) — both work
- Captions ON (critical for LinkedIn feed scrolling)

**Post Copy:**
- Hook in first line (grab attention before "…see more")
- Context (what's the video about?)
- CTA (comment, share, or click link)

**Boost Strategy:**
- Promote to followers first (day 1-2)
- Boost with $50-$100 if it performs well (engagement rate >3%)

---

### Website Embedding

**Where to embed:**
- Homepage hero section (explainer video)
- Product pages (demo videos)
- Case study pages (testimonial videos)
- Blog posts (tutorial videos)

**Best practices:**
- Use YouTube or Vimeo embed (not self-hosted — better load times)
- Add thumbnail with play button (don't auto-play)
- Lazy-load video (improves page speed)
- Include transcript below video (SEO + accessibility)
