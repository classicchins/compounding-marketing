# Product Launch Planning

Plan and execute successful product launches.

## Launch Tiers

**Major Launch:** New product, major feature
- 8+ weeks prep
- Multi-channel
- PR, partnerships, content

**Minor Launch:** Feature improvement
- 2-4 weeks prep
- Email + blog
- Existing channels

**Update:** Bug fix, small improvement
- Changelog only

## Process

### Step 1: Define Launch Tier & Goals
**Goals:**
- [Metric + target]: e.g., "1,000 signups in Week 1"

### Step 2: Build Timeline

**8 Weeks Before:**
- Define positioning and messaging
- Create launch assets (blog, video, graphics)
- Reach out to partners/press

**4 Weeks Before:**
- Finalize content
- Schedule emails
- Prepare social posts
- Brief sales/support teams

**2 Weeks Before:**
- QA all materials
- Confirm PR placements
- Tease on social
- Send preview to VIP customers

**1 Week Before:**
- Final review
- Load content (don't publish yet)
- Send launch email to team

**Launch Day:**
- Publish blog post
- Send email to list
- Post on social
- Submit to Product Hunt, Hacker News, etc.
- Activate paid ads
- Monitor and engage

**Post-Launch (Week 1-4):**
- Analyze metrics
- Gather feedback
- Iterate messaging
- Follow up with leads
