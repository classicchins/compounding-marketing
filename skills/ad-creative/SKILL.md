# Ad Creative & Copywriting

Write high-performing ad copy for paid campaigns.

## Platform Best Practices

**Google Search Ads:**
- Headline: Include keyword + benefit
- Description: CTA + differentiator
- Extensions: Sitelinks, callouts

**Facebook/Instagram Ads:**
- Hook: First line grabs attention
- Visual: High-quality image/video
- CTA: Clear button ("Learn More", "Sign Up")

**LinkedIn Ads:**
- Professional tone
- B2B value prop
- Lead gen forms (reduce friction)

## Ad Copywriting Framework

### Headline
- Include keyword (Google)
- Promise benefit
- Create curiosity

### Body
- Problem + solution
- Social proof (if space)
- Clear CTA

### Visual
- Product in action
- Before/after
- Customer using product

## Process

### Step 1: Research
- What competitors are running? (Facebook Ad Library)
- What messaging resonates?

### Step 2: Write Variations (3-5)
- Test different hooks
- Test different CTAs

### Step 3: Design Creative
- Match ad copy
- Platform specs (size, format)

## Output
Ad copy variations + creative concepts.

## Creative Frameworks: High-Converting Formulas

### Video Ads: First 3 Seconds Rule

**80% of viewers drop off after 3 seconds.** Your hook determines everything.

#### Hook Formulas for Video Ads

**1. Pattern Interrupt (Stop the scroll)**
- Visual: Unexpected movement (hand waving, object dropping)
- Audio: Loud sound, voice saying "Wait!"
- Text overlay: Bold claim or question

**Example (B2B SaaS):**
- [0-3s] Screen shows: "We spent $50K on ads and got ZERO customers"
- [3-5s] "Here's what we learned..."

---

**2. Problem Agitation (Make them feel the pain)**
- [0-3s] Show the problem visually (frustrated person, messy spreadsheet)
- [3-5s] "Tired of [pain point]?"

**Example (Project Management):**
- [0-3s] Split screen: chaotic Slack threads, missed deadlines
- [3-5s] "Managing a remote team shouldn't feel like herding cats"

---

**3. Results-First (Lead with the win)**
- [0-3s] Show the outcome (number, stat, before/after)
- [3-5s] "Here's how we did it"

**Example (Marketing Tool):**
- [0-3s] "We 3x'd our email open rates in 30 days"
- [3-5s] "This is the one change we made..."

---

**4. Question Hook (Curiosity gap)**
- [0-3s] Ask a provocative question
- [3-5s] Tease the answer

**Example (Analytics Tool):**
- [0-3s] "Are you tracking the metric that actually predicts churn?"
- [3-5s] "Most SaaS companies aren't. Here's what you should watch instead..."

---

### Static Ad Frameworks (Image + Text)

#### 1. Problem/Solution (Classic)

**Layout:**
- **Left side:** Problem (dark, chaotic, frustrated)
- **Right side:** Solution (bright, organized, happy)
- **Arrow or "vs"** in the middle

**Copy:**
- Headline: "Stop [doing X the hard way]. Start [doing X the easy way]."
- Body: "With [Product], you can [benefit] in [timeframe]."
- CTA: "Try Free"

**Example (Email Tool):**
- Left: Messy inbox, stressed person
- Right: Clean inbox, relaxed person
- Headline: "Stop drowning in email. Start reaching inbox zero daily."

---

#### 2. Before/After (Social Proof)

**Layout:**
- **Before:** Metric or state (low, messy, slow)
- **After:** Improved metric or state (high, clean, fast)
- **Visual:** Graph, screenshot, or testimonial quote

**Copy:**
- Headline: "[Customer] went from [Before] to [After] in [Timeframe]"
- Body: "See how they did it"
- CTA: "Read Case Study"

**Example (SEO Tool):**
- Before: "50 visitors/month"
- After: "5,000 visitors/month"
- Headline: "How Acme Corp 100x'd their traffic in 6 months"

---

#### 3. Social Proof (Testimonial-Driven)

**Layout:**
- **Big quote:** Customer testimonial (2-3 sentences max)
- **Photo:** Customer headshot + logo
- **Stat:** If available (e.g., "↑230% revenue")

**Copy:**
- Headline: Customer quote
- Body: "[Customer Name], [Job Title] at [Company]"
- CTA: "Join 10,000+ teams using [Product]"

**Example (CRM):**
- Quote: "This CRM saved us 15 hours a week. We'll never go back to spreadsheets."
- Photo: Sarah Johnson, VP Sales at TechCorp
- CTA: "Start Free Trial"

### Copy Formulas Per Platform

#### Facebook/Instagram Headline Limits

- **Headline:** 40 characters (gets cut off otherwise)
- **Primary text:** 125 characters (before "See More")
- **Description:** 30 characters

**Best practice:** Front-load value in first 125 characters (that's all mobile users see).

**Example:**

**❌ Too long (gets cut off):**
> "Are you struggling to manage your remote team? We built a tool that helps..."

**✅ Optimized (under 125 chars):**
> "Manage remote teams 3x faster. No more chaotic Slack threads. Try free: [link]"

---

#### Google Search Ads (RSA - Responsive Search Ads)

**How it works:**
- You provide up to 15 headlines + 4 descriptions
- Google auto-rotates combinations to find best-performing mix

**Headline rules:**
- Max 30 characters per headline
- At least 3 headlines must include primary keyword
- Vary headlines (don't repeat same message 15 times)

**Example (Project Management Software):**

**Headlines:**
1. "Project Management for Teams"
2. "Collaborate in Real-Time"
3. "Try Free for 14 Days"
4. "Trusted by 10,000+ Companies"
5. "No Credit Card Required"
6. "Gantt Charts + Time Tracking"
7. "Built for Remote Teams"
8. "Integrate with Slack, Asana"
9. "Get Started in 5 Minutes"
10. "Save 15 Hours Per Week"

**Descriptions (90 chars each):**
1. "Manage projects, track time, and collaborate—all in one place. Start free today."
2. "Join 10,000+ teams using ProjectPlanner to ship faster. No setup fees, cancel anytime."

---

#### LinkedIn Ads (Professional Tone)

**Best practices:**
- Skip the emoji (unless brand-appropriate)
- Lead with business outcome (ROI, efficiency, growth)
- Use stats ("3x faster" not "really fast")

**Example:**

**❌ Too casual (wrong for LinkedIn):**
> "OMG, this tool is 🔥! Try it and see the magic ✨"

**✅ Professional:**
> "Marketing teams using [Product] close 40% more deals. See how: [link]"

### Creative Testing Matrix (What to Test)

Test **one variable at a time** (not everything at once).

| Test Variable | Option A | Option B | Metric to Track |
|---------------|----------|----------|-----------------|
| **Visual** | Product screenshot | Customer photo | CTR (click-through rate) |
| **Copy Angle** | Problem-focused | Benefit-focused | CTR |
| **CTA** | "Start Free Trial" | "Get Demo" | Conversion rate |
| **Offer** | 14-day trial | 30-day trial | Conversion rate |
| **Headline** | Question ("Are you...?") | Statement ("Stop...") | CTR |
| **Format** | Single image | Carousel | Engagement rate |

**How to run test:**
1. Split budget 50/50 between A and B
2. Run for 7 days or 1,000 impressions (whichever comes first)
3. Declare winner (based on CTR or conversion rate)
4. Turn off loser, scale winner

### Creative Fatigue Signals (When to Refresh)

**Your creative is "fatigued" when:**

| Signal | Threshold | What It Means | Action |
|--------|-----------|---------------|--------|
| **CTR drops >30%** | Was 2%, now <1.4% | People have seen it too many times | Refresh creative |
| **CPM increases >50%** | Was $10, now $15+ | Platform penalizing repetitive ad | Pause, create new ad |
| **Frequency >5** | Same person sees ad 5+ times | Overexposure (annoying) | Rotate to new audience OR new creative |
| **Engagement drops** | Likes/comments declining | Audience bored | Change visual OR copy angle |

**Refresh triggers:**
- **Every 2-4 weeks** (for high-spend campaigns)
- **When CTR drops >30%** (for any campaign)
- **Seasonal changes** (Q4 holiday vs. Q1 new year messaging)

**How to refresh without starting over:**
- Change the image (keep copy the same)
- Change the headline (keep visual the same)
- Change the angle (problem-focused → benefit-focused)
- Add urgency ("Limited time" if it's a real deadline)

---

## Quality Bar for Creative Frameworks

- **Video ads:** Hook in first 3 seconds (pattern interrupt, problem agitation, or results-first)
- **Static ads:** Clear framework used (Problem/Solution, Before/After, or Social Proof)
- **Copy:** Platform-appropriate (125 chars for Facebook, 30 chars for Google headlines)
- **Testing plan:** One variable tested at a time (visual, copy, CTA, offer)
- **Refresh schedule:** New creative every 2-4 weeks OR when CTR drops >30%
