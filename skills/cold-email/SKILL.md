# Cold Email Outreach

Write personalized, high-converting cold emails.

## Framework (PAS)
**Problem:** Identify their pain
**Agitate:** Make it visceral
**Solution:** Position your product

## Structure
**Subject:** Personalized + intriguing
**Line 1:** Personalization (show you researched)
**Lines 2-3:** Problem + agitate
**Lines 4-5:** Solution (brief)
**CTA:** Clear next step (not "let me know")

## Example
```
Subject: Quick question about [Their Company]'s [Pain Point]

Hi [Name],

I noticed [Specific observation about their company/role].

Most [their role] struggle with [pain] — it leads to [agitation].

We help [similar companies] [solve pain] by [brief how].

Worth a 15-min call? [Calendar link]

[Your Name]
```

## Quality Bar
- Personalized (not template-obvious)
- Value-first (not pitch-first)
- Short (<100 words)
- Clear CTA
- No "just checking in" follow-ups
