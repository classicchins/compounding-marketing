# AI Search Optimization (AEO/GEO)

Optimize content for AI answer engines (ChatGPT, Perplexity, Google SGE).

## Key Principles

### 1. Structured Data
- Schema markup for entities
- Clear headings and sections
- FAQ schema for Q&A content

### 2. Concise, Factual Answers
- Answer questions directly (first 100 words)
- Use bulleted lists
- Include statistics and data

### 3. Cite-able Content
- Be an authoritative source
- Link to primary research
- Include author bios and credentials

### 4. Natural Language
- Write how people ask questions
- Use conversational tone
- Include question-based headings ("What is...", "How to...")

## Process

### Step 1: Identify Questions
- What questions does your audience ask?
- Use People Also Ask, forums, interviews

### Step 2: Structure Content
```markdown
## [Question as H2]

**Quick Answer:** [1-2 sentence direct answer]

**Detailed Explanation:** [Expanded answer with examples]

**Related:** [Links to related questions]
```

### Step 3: Add Schema
- FAQ schema for Q&A pages
- HowTo schema for guides
- Organization schema for about pages

## Output

Provide AI-optimized content structure with direct answers, schema markup recommendations, and cite-able formatting.

## Quality Bar
- Answers questions directly (no fluff)
- Structured data implemented
- Clear authority signals
