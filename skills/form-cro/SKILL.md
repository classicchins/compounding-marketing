# Form Optimization

Optimize forms for higher completion rates.

## Best Practices
1. **Fewer fields** (each adds friction)
2. **Single column** (easier to scan)
3. **Clear labels** (above or inside field)
4. **Smart defaults** (reduce typing)
5. **Helpful errors** (inline validation)
6. **Action button** ("Get My Demo" not "Submit")
7. **Privacy note** (reduce anxiety)

## Audit
- Field count (can any be removed or deferred?)
- Button copy (action-oriented?)
- Error handling (helpful?)
- Mobile experience (large touch targets?)

## Output
Provide optimized form design with copy, layout, and field recommendations.
