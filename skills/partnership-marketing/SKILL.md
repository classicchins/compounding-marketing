# Partnership Marketing

Leverage partnerships for mutual growth.

## Partnership Types

**Integration Partnerships:**
- Build integration with complementary product
- Co-market to both audiences

**Content Partnerships:**
- Co-create webinar, ebook, or guide
- Cross-promote to audiences

**Affiliate Partnerships:**
- Partners earn commission for referrals

**Reseller Partnerships:**
- Partners sell your product

## Process

### Step 1: Identify Potential Partners
**Criteria:**
- Complementary product (not competitor)
- Overlapping audience (same ICP)
- Similar brand values

**Examples:**
- "Project management + time tracking"
- "CRM + email marketing"

### Step 2: Define Value Exchange
**What you offer:**
- Access to your audience
- Integration/API support
- Co-marketing resources

**What they offer:**
- Same

**Equal value = successful partnership.**

### Step 3: Structure Deal
**Integration:**
- Build technical integration
- Joint launch plan
- Co-branded content

**Content:**
- Webinar (co-host)
- Ebook (co-author)
- Blog swap (guest posts)

**Affiliate:**
- Commission structure (% or flat fee)
- Tracking links
- Marketing materials

### Step 4: Promote
- Announce partnership (blog, email, social)
- Integration page on website
- Case studies (joint customers)

## Output
Partnership plan (targets, value exchange, structure, promotion).
